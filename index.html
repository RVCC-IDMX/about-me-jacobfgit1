Some Background on Me!

Hello! My name is Jacob. I was born in South Carolina and moved to New Jersey when I was three months old. I grew up in Hillsborough up until high school where I moved to the small town in Somerset Hills known as Bernardsville.

During Highschool I wasn't completely sure what I wanted to do in terms of career. Initially I was very interested in politics and political science as I liked to debate. After highschool I went to George Washington University in Washington D.C. to their business school studying International Business with a minor in political science.

Very quickly I realized I was not actually a big fan of political science nor International Business. I much prefered working with numbers and performing analysis. I changed my major to Finance and fell in love with my classes. Recently I have been interning with a Tax Accounting firm in Bethesda, MD that also has a consulting team that I will be joining full time after graduating this December.

I've always loved playing around with computers, i've been using them nearly everyday since elementary school. I wanted to take Web Development to get a small peek at coding. HTML and CSS have been a pretty interesting language to play around with, i'm excited to learn more about coding and software development.
